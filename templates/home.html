About the App:
This app was made during the Stem-Away Summer 2021 Virtual Internship. The main contributors of the team were: Caleb Kim, Sihong Yuan, Xinyue Ma, Lael Davis and Nilsu Duran. The project mentor was Sara El-Ateif.

How the App Works:
We scraped data from about 13,000 posts from the Drowned in Sound Community Forum. After many experiments, we settled on a model to create an embedding for each post. When the user inputs the post information (category, title, and body) and asks for recommendations, the app uses the aforementioned model to create an embedding for the inputted post, and then uses cosine similarity between the embedding for the inputted post and the 13,000 previously generated post embeddings to determine which 5 posts to display on the recommendations page.
